The Relay

Four games. Two teams. One frantic race.

Grab three friends, face off against a rival team of four, and sprint through four mini-games — you only advance when everyone on your team goes green. First team through Stage 4 wins the bragging rights.

How a relay works

No trivia, nothing to Google — every player gets their own board, and your hands do the answering.

1
🧩

Solve your puzzle

Everyone on the team gets their own randomly generated board.

2
🟢

Go green

Crack it and you're green — now you're waiting on your teammates.

3
⏱️

Survive the holding fire

Rest runs out? Quick-fire questions keep you honest. Miss one and you lose your green!

4
🚀

Advance together

All four green → next stage. First team through Stage 4 wins.
